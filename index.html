About me:

I did my undergraduate studies (M.Math.) at Oxford University in 2003-2007, and graduate studies (Ph.D.) at Cornell University in 2008-2013. My Ph.D. supervisor is Dan Barbasch.

My area of research is on representation theory of reductive Lie groups, a mathematical tool to describe many intriguing symmetries hidden in our physical world. More precisely, I use the atlas computer program to study the unitary dual of real reductive groups, as well as their p-adic counterparts.
