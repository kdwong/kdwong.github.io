About me:

I obtained my undergraduate degree at Wadham College, Oxford University in 2007, and graduate degree at Cornell University in 2013. My Ph.D. supervisor is Dan Barbasch.

My area of research is on representation theory of reductive Lie groups. More precisely, I use the atlas computer program to study the unitary dual of real reductive groups, as well as their p-adic counterparts.

You can find my list of publications below.

We have openings for Ph.D. students and postdoctoral fellows. Feel free to contact me if you are interested.
